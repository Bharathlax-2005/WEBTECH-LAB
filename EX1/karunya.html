Karunya University

About Karunya University

Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences, popularly known as Karunya University, is a private Christian university located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. It was founded in 1986 with the vision of providing quality education rooted in Christian values.

Academic Programs

Karunya University offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including engineering, science, business, arts, and social sciences.

Campus Facilities

The university campus is equipped with modern facilities, including state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, a library, hostels, and recreational areas. The campus provides a conducive environment for academic and personal development.

Mission and Values

The mission of Karunya University is to raise leaders and professionals with a strong sense of ethics and social responsibility. The university is committed to fostering a holistic learning experience that integrates faith and knowledge.
